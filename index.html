Neo Social Autopilot

Neo Social Autopilot is a private desktop creator tool used by TeddyJay4395 to schedule, manage, and publish owned stream clips to connected social platforms.

The tool is designed for personal creator workflow use. It allows the creator to select video clips, add titles, captions, and hashtags, choose publishing destinations, and upload content through official platform APIs such as YouTube and TikTok.

Neo Social Autopilot only publishes content chosen by the creator. The tool does not scrape TikTok, impersonate users, automate engagement, or post content owned by other people.

Current Use

Neo Social Autopilot is used to help manage short-form video posting for the TeddyJay4395 creator channels.
