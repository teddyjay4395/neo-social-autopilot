Neo Social Autopilot

Neo Social Autopilot is a desktop creator publishing tool that helps streamers and video creators prepare, schedule, and publish short-form clips they own to connected social platforms.

The workflow lets a creator select a video file, add a title, caption, and hashtags, choose a publishing destination, and submit the content through official platform APIs. For TikTok, Neo Social Autopilot uses TikTok Login Kit for user authorization and TikTok’s Content Posting API for creator-authorized video publishing.

Creator Workflow

Neo Social Autopilot is designed for creator-owned content. Creators choose the clips they want to upload, review their metadata, and schedule or submit posts from a local dashboard.

Clip selection Captions Hashtags Scheduling TikTok Login Kit Content Posting API

Platform Connections

Connected creator accounts are authorized through each platform’s official login and OAuth process. Neo Social Autopilot does not ask users to share platform passwords.

Responsible Publishing

Neo Social Autopilot is built for creator-selected uploads. It does not automate likes, comments, follows, scraping, or artificial engagement.
