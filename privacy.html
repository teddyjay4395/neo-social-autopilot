Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neo Social Autopilot handles data.

Neo Social Autopilot is a private desktop creator tool used by TeddyJay4395 to schedule and publish owned stream clips to connected social platforms.

1. Information the Tool Uses

Neo Social Autopilot may store or use the following information locally:

  • Selected video file paths
  • Video titles
  • Captions
  • Hashtags
  • Scheduled posting times
  • Posting history
  • Platform upload results
  • OAuth access or refresh tokens needed to connect approved creator accounts

2. How Information Is Used

This information is used to schedule posts, upload selected videos, show posting history, and connect to third-party platforms through official APIs.

3. Local Storage

Neo Social Autopilot stores app data locally on the creator’s computer. OAuth tokens may be stored locally so the creator does not need to reconnect the same account every time.

4. Third-Party Platforms

When the creator chooses to upload content to a third-party platform, relevant video files, captions, hashtags, and account authorization data may be sent to that platform through its official API.

Those platforms may process the uploaded content according to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. No Sale of Data

Neo Social Autopilot does not sell personal data.

6. No Public User Accounts

Neo Social Autopilot is not currently a public user platform. It does not provide public account registration, public profiles, or public user-to-user services.

7. Data Removal

Because Neo Social Autopilot stores data locally, the app owner can remove stored queue, history, upload, and token files from the local computer.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app owner through the TeddyJay4395 creator channels.
